About Blood Group Inheritance

Blood types are determined by multiple alleles. The ABO blood group is controlled by a single gene with three alleles: A, B, and O. The Rh factor (+ or -) is determined by a separate gene.

This tool provides a scientific prediction based on genetic inheritance patterns as defined by the World Health Organization standards.

Note: This tool provides predictions based on genetic inheritance patterns. For medical decisions, please consult healthcare professionals.
